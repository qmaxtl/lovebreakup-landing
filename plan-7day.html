This 7-day plan is designed to help you stabilize your emotions, gain clarity, and take the first step towards healing after a breakup.

Day 1: Acknowledge the Pain

  • Write down what you're feeling right now.
  • Speak to the AI therapist or journal for 10 minutes.

Day 2: Disconnect to Reconnect

  • Take a 24-hour social media break.
  • Turn off all notifications related to your ex.

Day 3: Reclaim Your Space

  • Remove photos, gifts, or messages that trigger pain.
  • Clean and organize your room or personal space.

Day 4: Reconnect with Yourself

  • Make a list of things you love doing alone.
  • Watch your favorite movie or go for a solo walk.

Day 5: Talk It Out

  • Have a conversation with the AI therapist about your progress.
  • Open up to one close friend or family member.

Day 6: Future Vision

  • Write a letter to your future self, 6 months from now.
  • Set 3 small goals you want to achieve this month.
